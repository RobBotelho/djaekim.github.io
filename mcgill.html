McGill

Protein Structure and Function Poster Presentation

With 3 other members, I researched the properties of Streptavidin, which made it ideal for numerous laboratory methods. We used molecular tool known as Pymol to map the structures of the protein obtained from NCBI - protein database. We presented the posters to top researchers in Structural Biology at McGill.

Literature Review

As a summer research student, under Professor Kalle Gehring and his graduate student Marjan, I researched elucidation of 3D structure of molecular complex formed my activated Parkin. Parkin is a U3 Ubiquitin protein ligase, targeting depolarized mitochondrial for proteasomal degradation. Parkin and defective ubiquitination is associated with Parkinson’s disease.

Biochemistry Formal Ball

Biochemistry Undergraduate Society, also known as BUGS, organized yearly Formal Ball for the graduating students. In 2016, as a senior representative of BUGS, I had an amazing opportunity to be the main executive of the event. After a long search for a beautiful, affordable venue, Montreal Fine Arts Museum was chosen. Formal Ball was an amazing turn out. Tremendous efforts had been made by our committee in organizing daily fundraiser to subsidize the costs, which made it affordable for students.
