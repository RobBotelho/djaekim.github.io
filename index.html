You Only Live Once. But, you get to serve twice

In university, when I was asked about my major and I jokingly replied, "I major in tennis". It was supposed to be humorous because I trained tennis for 2 hours everyday. I wasn't part of the varsity tennis team, but I was crazy in love with it.

I started tennis at the age of 10 and went through intesive training ever since. My father's first lesson was, "if you want to be good at something you have to put in 1000 hours."

He was an incredibly strict coach, critizing my mistakes without a moment’s hesitation. The most important message I received from tennis was success comes from patience, preparation and learning from failures.
